I've lived in the United States all my life.
I grew up in a suburb outside of Los Angeles called Chino Hills. I attended kindergarten through high school here.
After graduating from high school, I didn't go very far to attend college. I moved to Claremont, CA - a mere twenty minute drive from my hometown - and attended Scripps College.
I graduated in May, 2015 with a B.A. in Computational Neuroscience.

During my time at Scripps, I worked as a Research Assistant for the Decision Neuroscience Lab at Claremont McKenna College. It was here that I was exposed to electroencephalography and data analysis on brain data. My senior thesis was titled "Time-Frequency Analysis of Neural Oscillations in Multi-Attribute Decision Making".
I moved to San Francisco soon after graduating from Scripps and began my first job at SOASTA working as a Data Scientist. A few years after I joined the company, Akamai acquired SOASTA. I still live and work in San Francisco now, and couldn't love it more!
